Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie, It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

FEATURED IN: Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups(500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups(350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra fresh or frozen cranberries

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes(scrape rind off with a parking knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix togheter all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't nedd to actually knead the dough, thought I something knead it a few times in the bowls when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
